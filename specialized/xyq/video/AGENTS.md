## Prompt Rules

### Formula
```
[Subject] + [Motion] + [Scene] + [Camera movement] + [Lighting] + [Duration] + [Style]
```

### Camera movements
| Movement | Effect | Keywords |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Dolly In | Zoom in, focus detail | slow push in, move to close-up |
| Dolly Out | Zoom out, reveal environment | slow pull back, reveal full view |
| Pan | Rotate left/right | pan from left to right |
| Truck | Parallel move | track sideways, parallel follow |
| Pedestal Up | Rise up | rise from ground, crane up |
| Pedestal Down | Descend | descend from sky, lower down |
| Tracking | Follow subject | follow behind, keep subject center |
| Orbit | 360° around subject | orbit around, 360 rotation |
| Handheld | Shaky, documentary feel | handheld, slight camera shake |
